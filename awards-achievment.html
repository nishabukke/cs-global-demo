Our Journey of Excellence: Awards and Achievements

Our dedication to excellence at iSource India has been consistently recognized through numerous prestigious awards and achievements. These accolades reflect our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction. We take pride in being acknowledged by industry leaders for our outstanding contributions and relentless pursuit of perfection. Each award is a testament to our team's hard work, expertise, and the trust our clients place in us. As we continue to set higher standards, we remain motivated to deliver exceptional solutions and maintain our position as industry frontrunners.

Ganapati Bappa: Crafted from Indian Parts for a US-Based Customer

At iSource India, we take immense pride in showcasing our contribution to developing a unique Ganapati Bappa idol for a US-based customer, made entirely from parts sourced and manufactured in India. This project highlights not only our dedication to delivering quality but also our expertise in managing complex supply chains across borders.

From the initial feasibility study, we ensured that each component aligned with the customer's requirements. We meticulously identified suitable suppliers across India, focusing on both craftsmanship and quality. Our team expertly handled negotiations, ensuring cost-efficiency without compromising on standards.

With this project, iSource India reinforced its capability to seamlessly deliver intricate, high-quality products while managing every stage of the sourcing process for international clients. Ganapati Bappa, crafted from Indian parts, stands as a symbol of our commitment to excellence and collaboration across borders.

Business Coach Rajiv Talareja Inaugurates Our E-Book on Strategic Sourcing at MBA Event in Mumbai

We are thrilled to announce the inauguration of our latest e-book, "Steps in Strategic Sourcing," by none other than our esteemed business coach, Rajiv Talareja, at a prestigious MBA event in Mumbai. It was a great honor to be recognized at such a platform, and having Rajiv Talareja unveil our e-book added an extra layer of significance to this milestone.

This e-book is the culmination of our extensive experience in sourcing, carefully drafted to guide professionals and students alike through the intricate process of strategic sourcing. The insights provided within are a reflection of the knowledge we’ve accumulated over the years, offering practical steps for success in the field.

A Proud Milestone: Meher Pudumjee of Thermax Limited Inaugurates iSource India

We are deeply honored to announce that Meher Pudumjee, Chairperson of Thermax Limited, inaugurated our Procurement and Sourcing Business. This moment marks a significant milestone in our journey, as it is not only a recognition of our growth but also a reflection of the strong foundations we built during our time at Thermax.

During the inauguration, we had an insightful discussion about the key learnings from our experience at Thermax, which played a pivotal role in shaping the vision and mission of iSource India. Several incidents from our time there ignited the spark for launching this business, providing us with the expertise and drive to succeed in the global sourcing industry.

A Milestone in My Professional Journey: Advanced Programme in Supply Chain Management from IIM Calcutta

I am thrilled to share a proud achievement in my professional journey – I have successfully completed the Advanced Programme in Supply Chain Management from the prestigious Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta (IIMC)!

As the Director of iSource India, this experience has been transformative, equipping me with advanced knowledge and tools to further enhance our capabilities in global procurement and sourcing. This program has pushed me to new heights, challenging my thought process, problem-solving abilities, and reinforcing the values of resilience and adaptability.

iSource India Recognized by THERMAX BABCOCK & WILCOX ENERGY SOLUTIONS LTD

We are honored to share that our team has been recognized by our esteemed client, THERMAX BABCOCK & WILCOX ENERGY SOLUTIONS LTD, for our contributions to the Expediting Assignment for the Deaerator Tank in the HRRL Project. Receiving this prestigious award is a testament to our commitment to excellence, teamwork, and project success. 🏆

We look forward to future opportunities to collaborate on Sourcing and Expediting Assignments, continuing to deliver value and expertise.

iSource India Team Appreciated for Efforts in India Sourcing at International Engineering Sourcing Show 2024

We are proud to share that the iSource India team was recognized for its contributions to India sourcing at the International Engineering Sourcing Show (IESS) 2024. Organized by the Engineering Export Promotion Council (EEPC), the event took place at Codissia on 4th and 5th March 2024, showcasing India’s strengths in smart manufacturing.

iSource India actively engaged with leading Indian suppliers and international buyers, establishing valuable connections for future sourcing endeavors. We extend our heartfelt thanks to the exhibitors and participants for making this event a great success.
