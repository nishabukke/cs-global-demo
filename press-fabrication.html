Press Parts & Fabrication Solutions by I Source

I Source excels in providing high-quality press parts and fabrication services, meeting diverse industry needs with precision and reliability.

I Source offers comprehensive services for sourcing a wide range of fabricated and sheet metal components and assemblies. Our meticulous selection process ensures that manufacturers with excellent tool and fixture development capabilities are chosen, following extensive audits to guarantee quality and reliability.

Additionally, our network of trusted suppliers specializes in various fabrication techniques, including laser cutting, bending, welding, and assembly. With a focus on precision and efficiency, we collaborate closely with our manufacturing partners to deliver custom solutions tailored to meet the unique requirements of each project. Whether you need simple brackets or complex assemblies, I Source is committed to providing top-notch fabricated and sheet metal components that meet the highest standards of quality and performance.
