Precision Casting and Forging Solutions by I Source

I Source delivers superior casting and forging solutions, forging the future with precision and strength.

I Source is your trusted ally for sourcing a diverse range of castings from India. All castings are meticulously machined on CNC machines and undergo surface treatment, ensuring impeccable in-process and final quality control.

Our partnered manufacturers boast advanced facilities and certifications to ISO 9001-2000 and TS16949 systems. Castings adhere to multiple international standards including ISO, BS, ASTM, ANSI, DIN, JIS, and more.
