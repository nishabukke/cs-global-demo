Casting and Forging Solutions by CS Global

CS Global delivers superior casting and forging solutions, forging the future with precision and strength.

Casting :

CS Global Sourcing serves as your premier solution for sourcing a diverse array of castings from India. Our comprehensive approach ensures that all castings are not only procured but also delivered fully machined on CNC machines with meticulous surface treatment, adhering precisely to customer specifications and requirements.

Partnering with us grants you access to a network of manufacturers in India who boast robust infrastructure and certifications to ISO 9001-2000 and TS16949 standards. Our sourced castings align with various international standards such as ISO, BS, ASTM, ANSI, DIN, JIS, among others. We guarantee punctual delivery and meet stringent quality criteria while maintaining competitive costs. Experience seamless sourcing of castings from India through our carefully selected sources.

Forging :

CS Global Sourcing specializes in offering an extensive range of metal forgings sourced from India. These forgings are available in both raw form and fully machined on CNC machines, ensuring high accuracies and proactive quality assurance at every phase of production.

Our sourced forgings adhere to various international standards such as ASME, DIN, BS, JIS, ISO, among others. We guarantee timely delivery and meet stringent quality requirements while maintaining competitive costs. Experience seamless sourcing of forgings from India through our carefully selected sources.

Casting Solutions

In addition to our casting solutions, we offer a comprehensive suite of surface finishing options to enhance the quality and durability of your products:

  • Shot blasting
  • Thumb blasting
  • Vibro Deburring
  • Vacuum Impregnation
  • Teflon coating
  • Liquid Painting (including lead-free painting suitable for food applications)
  • Powder Coating

Moreover, to ensure the highest standards of quality control, we can facilitate third-party lab testing of test bars as an independent verification measure, supplementing the foundry test certificates provided.
