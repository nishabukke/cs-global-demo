iSource India Participates in the International Engineering Sourcing Show 2024

We are excited to share that iSource India had the privilege of being invited to the International Engineering Sourcing Show (IESS), organized by the Engineering Export Promotion Council (EEPC), held at Codissia on 4th and 5th March 2024. This event served as a fantastic platform for fostering connections between Indian exhibitors and international buyers, promoting India as a hub for smart manufacturing.

We are grateful to the exhibitors for making this event a tremendous success. As a sourcing company from India, we had the opportunity to engage with numerous Indian suppliers at the exhibition, building valuable relationships for future sourcing needs.

The event, supported by the Department of Commerce, Government of India, and EEPC India, was truly a one-of-a-kind experience that showcased India’s growing expertise in engineering and manufacturing on a global scale.

iSource India Participates in International Procurement and Supply Chain Conference 2024, Mumbai

We are pleased to share that the iSource India team was invited by Blue Ocean Corporation, UAE, to attend the prestigious International Procurement and Supply Chain Conference (IPSC) 2024 on June 1st at the Taj Lands End, Mumbai. With Sourav Ganguly as the chief guest, the event brought together global leaders from logistics, procurement, and supply chain sectors.

IPSC 2024 highlighted India's growing role in economic innovation and the importance of supply chain transformation. Since its inception in 2014, IPSC has been a key platform for advancing procurement and supply chain excellence.

We are honored to have been part of this impactful event and look forward to contributing further to the supply chain ecosystem.

Industrial Advisory Board at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune

We are proud to announce that our Director was recently invited to join the Industrial Advisory Board at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune—a proud moment as an alumnus!🌟

In discussions with the institute's professors, we gained valuable insights into key supply chain management objectives, including:

📌 Key Objectives:

✨ Optimizing Efficiency, Minimizing Costs, Enhancing Customer Satisfaction

🏆 Competitive Advantage, Streamlining Logistics, Improving Delivery and Coordination

📈 Accurate Demand Forecasting, Strengthening Supplier Relationships, Ensuring Quality Control

🌱 Promoting Sustainability, Reducing Lead Times

These insights will help refine our supply chain processes at iSource India, and we deeply appreciate the contributions from these academic experts.

Meher Pudumjee: Inaugurated our Sourcing Business.

We are delighted to share that Meher Pudumjee, Chairperson of Thermax Limited, officially inaugurated our Industrial Procurement and Sourcing Business. Her insightful contributions on procurement strategies enriched our team's knowledge and provided fresh perspectives.

During the event, we had the opportunity to discuss best practices in global sourcing and explore future collaboration opportunities. This exchange has greatly motivated our team to continue driving innovation and excellence in our services.

Partnering with a prestigious company like Thermax Limited is an honor, and this event has further strengthened our collaboration. Our sincere thanks to Meher Madam for gracing the occasion and making it truly memorable.

iSource India Participates in MVPM International Connect: A Valuable Learning Experience

The MVPM International Connect session on "5 Key Steps to Establish Your International Business," led by Adv. Jayprakashji B Somani, was an enriching experience. It covered key topics such as the scope of international business, essential steps to establish IB, importance of documentation, international finance strategies, and global marketing.

iSource India was proud to participate and assist in organizing this event. These sessions help us stay updated on global trends and enhance our expertise in procurement and sourcing.
