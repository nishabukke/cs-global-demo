Precision Machining by I Source

At I Source, precision machining ensures unparalleled accuracy and quality for your intricate engineering needs.

I Source specializes in the supply of various types of precision machined components, sourced from manufacturers rigorously audited for their quality management systems and certified to ISO9001:2000, TS16949, and AS9000 standards.

Our materials range from stainless steel, low and high carbon steel, aluminum, brass, copper, to exotic materials like hastelloy, adhering to ASTM, ISO, DIN, JIS, and MIL standards. Additionally, we offer a variety of surface treatments including heat treatment, plating of nickel, chrome, silver, plasma coating, ED coating, polyester and epoxy coating, lead-free painting, and conductive painting, among others.
