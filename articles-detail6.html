GLOBAL PROCUREMENT

Our Unique Sourcing Process: Simplifying Global Procurement

At iSource India, we follow a structured and comprehensive sourcing process designed to meet your business needs while ensuring quality, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness. Our approach involves close collaboration with clients and suppliers, delivering unmatched value at every step. Here’s an overview of our unique sourcing process:

1. Define

We start by understanding your specific requirements and business goals to ensure we deliver exactly what you need.

2. Analyze

Our team performs an external market analysis, identifying gaps and opportunities in the sourcing landscape to provide the best options.

3. Strategize

We formulate a *custom sourcing strategy* tailored to your business, alongside a supplier development plan for sustainable partnerships.

4. Source

We evaluate potential suppliers based on *total cost of ownership (TCO)*, maintaining active communication with customers to ensure the best supplier selection.

5. Transact

Our team facilitates FAI (First Article Inspection) approvals, provides ongoing support during serial orders, and resolves any supplier-related queries swiftly.

6. Monitor

We maintain long-term relationships by managing Supplier Relationship Management (SRM) and a Supplier Score Board, continuously monitoring for improvement.

With our transparent sourcing process, iSource India ensures you receive top-quality products, timely deliveries, and competitive pricing—allowing your business to thrive in global markets.
