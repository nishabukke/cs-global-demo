FAQ: Everything You Need to Know

At iSource India, we understand that you may have questions about our services, processes, and capabilities. To assist you, we have compiled a list of Frequently Asked Questions that provide detailed answers and insights into our operations. Whether you are curious about our engineering solutions, project timelines, or quality assurance measures, our FAQ section is designed to address your queries and help you make informed decisions. If you need further assistance, our team is always here to provide personalized support and guidance.

At iSource India, we offer end-to-end product sourcing services. This includes identifying reliable suppliers, conducting quality checks, managing negotiations, and overseeing logistics for consumer, technical, and industrial products. We also provide customized sourcing solutions to fit your specific requirements.

We have a stringent quality assurance process that includes multiple rounds of inspection during production. Our team conducts factory audits, reviews supplier capabilities, and performs pre-shipment quality checks to ensure all products meet international quality standards before delivery.

Yes, we specialize in custom product development and private labeling. Whether you need a specific design, branding, or product customization, our team works closely with manufacturers to deliver exactly what you need. We help you bring your ideas to life while maintaining cost efficiency.

iSource India is experienced in sourcing a wide variety of products across industries such as consumer goods, technical products, and industrial components. We specialize in sectors including Raw Material Sourcing , Automotive, Industrial Fabrication , Pre Engineered Buildings , Manufactucturing , Machinery, and more.

We follow a comprehensive supplier verification process. This includes detailed factory audits, assessments of production capacity, and background checks to ensure that suppliers adhere to international manufacturing standards. We partner only with manufacturers who meet our strict criteria for quality, reliability, and compliance.

Yes, we offer complete logistics support. This includes coordination of shipping, handling customs clearance, and managing the entire supply chain process. Whether you need products delivered locally or internationally, we ensure timely and hassle-free shipping to your desired destination.

We ensure that all products sourced through iSource India meet the necessary international regulations and certifications. Depending on the market and industry, we guarantee compliance with certifications such as ISO, CE, RoHS, and other relevant global standards.

Our commitment to your organization does not end when an alliance partner is selected. Rather, Isource meets the long term strategic initiatives of its clients by finding the people and facilities best suited to their needs and then staying involved to monitor the continuous improvement process.

We prioritize open communication with our clients throughout the entire sourcing process. Regular updates are provided on the status of production, quality checks, shipping, and delivery schedules via email or other preferred channels. We ensure complete transparency at every step to build trust and confidence.

Timelines vary depending on the complexity and type of product, as well as the production cycle of the manufacturer. Generally, it takes around 4-12 weeks from the start of sourcing to delivery. We work closely with clients to meet project deadlines and keep you informed throughout the process.
